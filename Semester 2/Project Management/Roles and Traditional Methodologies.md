# Project Stakeholder Management
## Stakeholder Management
- Identify
- Analyze expectations and impact on project
- Develop appropriate management strategies

## Stakeholder Register
- Used throughout project
- Manage interactions with stakeholders

### Identify Stakeholders
- Top Managers
- Your Manager
- Peers
- Resource Managers
- Internal/External Customers
- Government
- Contractors, Subcontractors, Suppliers

### Stakeholder Analysis
- Who are they?
- What are their interests?
- Will their interest level vary?
- Can coalitions be built

### Project Sponsor
- Person or group responsible for enabling success
- Usually outside the project
- Signs off that project is complete

### Politics of Project
- The environment
- The goals of each stakeholder
- Power

## Cultural Influences
- Communication

- Take ownership

- Stand by decisions
- Take accountability

## Rate of Return
(Total benefits - Total Costs) / Total Costs

- Can be used to compare different options
- Organization may have a minimum acceptable rate of return for projects

## Payback Analysis
- Compares cumulative costs to cumulative benefits
- Easiest to see in graphical format
