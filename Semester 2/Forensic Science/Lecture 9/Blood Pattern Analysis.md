# Composition of Blood
- 55% plasma
	- ~0.5% solutes
	- ~4.0% proteins
	- ~50.5% water
- 1% Buffy Coat
	- ~0.5% platelets
	- ~0.5% leukocytes
- 44% Red Blood Cells
	- 44% erythrocytes

## Spectroscopy
Seeing what something is made off given the light it emits when blasted with electromagnetic waves
### Visible Absorbance Spectroscopy
![[Pasted image 20260417123756.png]]
- Samples with visible colour can be characterized and quantified
- Depending on the present molecules, changes in absorbance at specific wavelengths give us info about the sample

- Fresh blood is bright red as it absorbs oxygen from the environment
- Spectrum of oxyHb has characteristic absorbance bands

- Aged blood are brown/black. As the hemoglobin degrades, the oxygen is lost
- The spectrum of metHb has strong absorbance between 600-800 nm
