# The CSI Effect
While TV shows have brought much awareness to the worlds of forensic science, it has also made unrealistic expectations of CSI work.
- Different tasks are delegated to different people
- Processes which take days would actually take weeks, moths or YEARS

# Basic Services of "Full-Service" Crime Labs
- Physical science
	- Chemical tests
	- drugs
	- glass
	- paint
	- soil
- Biology Unit
	- DNA
	- Bodily fluids
	- Plants
- Firearms Unit
	- Ammunition
	- Weapons
	- Clothing for residue
- Document Examination unit
	- Documents
	- marks
	- writing
	- document burns
- Photography Unit
	- Records physical unit
	- all kinds of photography with different wavelength radiation

## Other Services
- Toxicology unit
	- Bodily fluids for drugs
- Latent Fingerprint Unit
	- To find latent Fingerprints
- Polygraph Unit
	- Lie detector
- Voiceprint Analysis Unit
	- To tie the voice of a particular suspect to the person
- Crime Scene Investigation Unit
	- Dispatches trained personnel to recover and preserve physical evidence
